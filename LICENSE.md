HỆ THỐNG TIN - SINH HỌC NHẬN DIỆN BIỂU CẢM VÀ TƯ THẾ HỌC SINH is a software application developed as an educational
and scientific research project by students of THPT Nguyễn Trãi.
The software applies artificial intelligence techniques to analyze facial emotions
and sitting posture in real time, with the purpose of supporting learning supervision
and improving students’ study awareness in school environments.

This software is developed under the guidance of teachers at THPT Nguyễn Trãi
and is intended strictly for educational and academic research purposes.
